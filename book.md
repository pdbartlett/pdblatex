### Starters

#### Frankie & Benny's Flatbread

##### Ingredients

* 250g plain flour
* 125ml water
* 2 1/2 tbsp oil
* pinch of salt

##### Method

1. Combined ingredients in a bowl
1. Split into approx. 6 pieces
1. Roll out each piece thinly and dry fry
